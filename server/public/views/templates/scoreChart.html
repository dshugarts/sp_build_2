How Do I Earn Points?

Age:

Low Risk (+2 Points): Men 45 and under and Women 55 and under

High Risk (+0 Points): Men over 45 and Women over 55

Nicotine:

Low Risk (+2 Points): Not a Nicotine User

High Risk (+0 Points): Nicotine User

Blood Pressure:

Low Risk (+2 Points): Systolic below 131 mmHg and Diastolic below 81 mmHg

Moderate Risk (+1 Point): Systolic 131-179 mmHg and Diastolic 81-119 mmHg

High Risk (+0 Points): Systolic 180+ mmHg and Diastolic 120+ mmHg

Fasting Glucose:

Low Risk (+2 Points): Under 100 mg/dL

Moderate Risk (+1 Point): Between 100 and 125 mg/dL

High Risk (+0 Points): Over 125 mg/dL

Waist Size (in.):

Low Risk (+2 Points): Men under 40in. and Women under 35in.

High Risk (+0 Points): Men over 40in. and Women over 35in.

HDL:

Low Risk (+2 Points): 60+ mg/dL

Moderate Risk (+1 Point): 41-59 mg/dL

High Risk (+0 Points): Men under 41 mg/dL and Women under 51 mg/dL

LDL:

Low Risk (+2 Points): Under 100 mg/dL

Moderate Risk (+1 Point): 130-159 mg/dL

High Risk (+0 Points): Over 159 mg/dL

Family History:

Low Risk (+2 Points): Father NOT diagnosed with CVD before 55 and Mother NOT diagnosed with CVD before 65

High Risk (+0 Points): Father diagnosed with CVD before 55 or Mother diagnosed with CVD before 65

Physical Activity:

Low Risk (+2 Points): Moderate physical activity over 150 min/week

High Risk (+0 Points): Moderate physical activity under 150 min/week

Sleep:

Low Risk (+2 Points): Sleeping 7+ hours/night

High Risk (+0 Points): Sleeping under 7 hours/night
